About

GA Infotech is an Atlanta based information technology company. We specialize in CRM implementations and also do custom software development for small and medium sized businesses.

With years of hands-on IT experience, we have a team of technical consultants, architects, software developers and project management professionals with specific interests in using technology to organize information and improve processes that help businesses grow.

Our implementations encompass a wide variety of business applications. At GA Infotech we strongly believe in agile methodologies and strive to implement simple solutions for complicated problems. We use well-proven design principles in conjunction with new and emerging technologies for architecting all our solutions.

Please feel free to reach out and contact us for more information.
