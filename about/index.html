Our Heritage

Georgia Roots. Worldwide Reach.

Established and based in Atlanta, Georgia, GA Infotech emerged out of a clear enterprise need: the necessity for honest, senior technical advisory that understands both business operations and complex custom software development.

We realized that too many business intelligence initiatives and CRM implementations fail not due to budget limitations, but due to poor architecture and high legacy friction. Our founder, Harpal Gujral, envisioned a firm that acts as a direct strategic partner—collaborating closely with executives to clear technical bottlenecks and deliver high-impact results with agile milestone precision.

Today, GA Infotech coordinates a world-class team of technical consultants, software developers, solution architects, and database administrators, managing continuous multi-channel CRM, custom enterprise applications, and outsourced operations with predictable excellence.

Core Philosophy

Simple solutions for complicated problems.

Over-engineering is the silent killer of enterprise software. When codebases and system dependencies become bloated, routine adjustments begin to demand massive, multi-week release cycles and inflated TCO.

At GA Infotech, we prioritize clean, maintainable microservice patterns, well-documented API triggers, and proven industry frameworks. By choosing stability over hype, we ensure our clients remain highly adaptive and scale with absolute security.
